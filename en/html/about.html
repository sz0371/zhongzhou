About us

        Zhejiang Z-Light Optoelectronics Co.,Ltd is located in the national development zone - Yuhang Economic Development Zone,Hangzhou,China.Which is specialized in R&D,manufacturing and selling high quality and high luminous efficiency LED lighting.Then company was established in March 2003,company registered capital of 165 million yuan and company covers area of 50 acres,27,000 square meters production base,complete matching facilities.The company has advanced,scientific and perfect management,and products are well known all over the world.
        Since the company established,the vision is "to be navigator of LED lighting in the 21th century",the enterprise mission is "to create a better world for lighting".Z-Light is focus on enterprise management,in accordance with the demands of the development of modern high-tech enterprise,company found a relatively perfect enterprise quality,production,technology,human resources and financial management system.The company passed ISO9001 and ROSH certification in 2005,TS/16949 in 2009.And the company also has UL,CE and other international and domestic certification.Z-Light is awarded the key enterprise of "Semiconductor Lighting Engineering"
The company has the leading and complete import and domestic LED products automatic production equipment,which can meet different customers needs.And the company also has different kinds of high specification,complete inspection and aging equipment,to ensure that every LED product produced by the company can meet customers high quality requirements.
        The company attaches importance to the technical innovation.We not only have the best talents in the industry to provide comprehensive and in-depth service to customers,but also have the provincial enterprise technology center which consisted of new material lab,encapsulation technology lab,reliability test lab,lighting product development lab and other professional R&D institutions.The company invest capital in R&D is more than 5% of revenue every year.The company also founded a joint R&D center with Zhejiang University Of Technology,and make the cooperation with the China Jiliang University ,Chinese Science President Changchun machinery,Beijing Research Institute of Chemical Industries,Hangzhou Normal University,to joint research on major technical problem.Rely on the abundant technical R&D strength,the company has undertaken the National 863 plan project,National torch plan project,National small and medium-sized enterprise innovation fund project,advanced manufacturer innovation LED project,Hangzhou science and technology innovation project and other projects.Got a series of scientific research achievements,the company has more than 60 authorization of patent through the past years.
        In recent years,with the continuous upgrading of LED technology,the products of Z-Light have been widely used in urban lighting,street lighting,indoor lighting and outdoor advertising.For example,the capital airport T2 terminal,Shanghai metro line 6,Beijing metro line 10,balcony city of Hangzhou,lights project on both side of Qiangtang river,Yuhang Qiushan tunnel project,industrial park street lighting project in Yuhang Tangxi and Huzhou Anji and some other big project.The Z-Light LED is glittering uniquely.
Our rapid development has also been fully affirmed by different levels of government. Also our company has been identified as "National Torch Plan Key High-Tech Enterprise", "Enterprise Focusing on Development in National Semiconductor Lighting Project", "National High-Tech Enterprise", "National Semiconductor Lighting Engineering R&D and Industry Alliance Member", "Top 100 Minor Enterprises in Zhejiang Province", "Science and Technology Start-up Enterprise Cultivated by Hangzhou City Government ", "Labor Relation Harmonious Demonstration Enterprise in Hangzhou City", "Production, Study & Research Demonstration Enterprise in Zhejiang Province", "Patent Demonstration Enterprise in Zhejiang Province", "Green, Low-Carbon, Economy Demonstration Enterprise in Zhejiang Province" and other honorary titles. Z-Light has been awarded as a famous trademark in Zhejiang province. The light emitting diodes produced by our company are recommended and widely used in Hangzhou municipal lighting project and landscape engineering.
        In the course of company development, we have met difficult and adversaries. In year 2008 the reform of shareholding completed, while in year 2013 we failed to independently public listed on stock market. Then we were in big troubles with normal operation. However, with our advance technology and good manufacture ability and unresisting spirit of our people Z-LIGHT was successfully reconstructed by an invest company under Xuzhou Handler Special Vehicle co.,ltd which is a stock list company. The reconstructed Z-LIGHT gain new vitality relying on the strong financial strength, rich resource advantage, standard operation and management of Xuzhou Handler Special Vehicle company brings and fund operation by its fund company..
Z-Light will continually uphold the corporation culture of responsibility, dedication, cooperation, pragmatic, innovation, and follow the corporation philosophy of business integrity, efficient service, striving for excellence and exploring. Our vision is to be a leader in lighting industry in 21 century. We will strive to become a world class optoelectronics company and create a beautiful lighting world for human being; meanwhile will make contribution to loft cause of low-carbon, energy saving, and emission reduction as well as to the well beings of all society.
